Our Journey

Before the Icing

It was the Pandemic of 2020 when God challenged Charmaine to learn how to give. Generosity is not her strong suit. She always struggle with giving, fearing that there will be nothing left for her if she gives. It was December when God impressed upon her to give gifts to her friends, but during that time she didn’t have much. All she had was baking ingredients, so she decided to bake cakes for them as a gift.

Buttercream Visions

She was craving something milky, creamy, and caramel-like. With a lot of time on her hands, she experimented on a Tres Leches recipe. And it turned out great! She was able to make salted caramel pecan Tres Leches. There was a lot left from the batch, and that’s what she gave out to her friends. Once they tasted it, they reached out to her instantly and asked if they could buy some and give it to their friends too.

All-Purpose Dream

At first, Charmaine didn’t want to; it was supposed to be a gift, not something she's going to make money out of. But after a month of convincing, she granted one request, then another, then another. Until she reached a point where she was making 5 cakes per day out of requests. That’s when she decided to leave customizing cakes and focus on making Tres Leches cakes instead. Because of generosity and obedience, Sugar Trends wouldn’t be here today.
